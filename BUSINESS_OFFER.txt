AUTOPILOT-AI BUSINESS OFFER

This document is internal planning material.
It is not wired into the app or the public site.

Core product promise
- Autopilot-AI turns Gmail and Calendar overload into a source-backed daily operating plan.
- It helps the user see what matters, block private senders from AI, draft replies, and protect time on the calendar.

Who this offer is for
- People with heavy inbox volume
- Founders
- Chiefs of staff
- Recruiters
- Operators
- Managers who work from Gmail and Calendar all day

BEGINNER PLAN
Price
- $5/month

Positioning
- The low-friction entry point.
- Gives the user the first real "aha" without overwhelming them.

Included
- Gmail sync
- Google Calendar sync
- Source-backed action extraction
- Daily action list
- Private sender AI blocking
- Basic calendar planning view
- Manual calendar blocks
- Limited reply draft generation
- Basic workspace customization

Limits
- Lower sync volume
- Fewer draft generations per day
- Fewer saved planning runs
- No premium onboarding

Best fit
- Students
- Solo founders
- Freelancers
- Budget-conscious first-time users

PLUS PLAN
Price
- $25/month

Positioning
- The serious individual operator plan.
- This should become the default paid tier.

Included
- Everything in Beginner
- Full AI daily planning
- Focus windows
- Quick capture
- Planning modes: Impact, Quick wins, Deep work
- Editable reply drafts with tone selection
- Assistant commands for draft generation and calendar blocks
- Action lab workflows
- Deeper workspace customization
- More sync volume
- More planning history

Best fit
- Operators
- Chiefs of staff
- Startup employees
- Recruiters
- Managers with constant inbound requests

PRO PLAN
Price
- $200/month

Positioning
- The high-trust, high-touch plan.
- Sell control, time savings, onboarding, and enterprise-style visibility.

Included
- Everything in Plus
- Executive command brief
- Approval-aware workflows
- Audit and usage visibility
- Organization policy controls
- Premium privacy posture
- High usage limits
- White-glove onboarding
- Fast support
- Hands-on setup help

Best fit
- Executives
- Agency owners
- Team leads
- Operators whose time is worth much more than the subscription

Packaging logic
- Beginner proves the core Gmail-to-action value fast.
- Plus captures the real everyday planning workflow.
- Pro sells trust, control, and leverage.

Recommended sales message
- Beginner: "Turn Gmail into a clean daily action list."
- Plus: "Let AI help run the day, not just sort the inbox."
- Pro: "Run your work like you have an AI operator with guardrails."

Recommended rollout
1. Lead with Plus as the default recommendation.
2. Keep Beginner as the easy yes.
3. Use Pro for high-touch customers who need trust and control.
